Who We Are

We are a charity organisation.
Our aim at Food Pacific is to ensure that more organic foods are grown(these foods include plants and animals) and these foods are distributed across poorer nations around east and west Africa.


All foods are processed under the best hygine conditions and are preserverd until it gets to the consumer There is no need for people to die of hunger in the 21st century when we can help in our own little ways. A person cannot help the community, but the community can help a person, and that is our motto at Food Pacific.
